Richland Library Northeast held two Community Conversations on October 15, 2015 to hear directly from local residents about what they value and need from their library. The intimate setting allowed people to voice their opinions with both library staff and the design team. Some topics that were covered: family space, children’s recreational space, furniture and parking. If you were not able to attend and would like to make a suggestion, please contact Jacki Sligh, at 803-736-6575.
